CrystalsCollector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by mathing your total score to the random number.
You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you. When you click a crystal the total score will increase and you will be have to figure out the value of each crystal.
Each time the game starts, the random number and values of the crystals will change.
